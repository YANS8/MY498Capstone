Curated drinks

Our wine and sake list covers old + new world favourites – many from small growers whom we know personally + have hosted wine dinners, to notes that perfectly suit our food. We love every bottle in our cellar.

And from our kitchen, we create incredible non-alcoholic drinks used in our house cocktails and pairings. These juices, kombuchas + ferments capture seasonal flavours at their best.

Please note that should you wish to bring your own bottles of wine that a corkage fee of £15 per person applies, with a maximum of 2 bottles allowed.
